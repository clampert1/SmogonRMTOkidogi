THREAT LIST
CORVIKNIGHT
Not many answers on this team. Brave Bird chip accumulates and starts winning games if not dealt with early. Mirror matchup is especially rough.
ARAQUANID (OFFENSIVE)
Offensive variant can pick up 1–2 KOs before the team can respond. Lots of chip + no immediate answer makes this especially threatening in the mid-game.
ALOMOMOLA
No immediate answers. Can usually be played around through smart pivoting, but if Alomomola stays healthy late-game it becomes an unkillable stall anchor.
